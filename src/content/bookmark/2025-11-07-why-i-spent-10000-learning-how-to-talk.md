When preparing to speak (for a talk or presentation) or when put on spot, we tend to focus on delivering the entire thing correctly. Instead, work on your first sentence (message and tone), then nail its delivery and let the rest flow. Similar to Steve's cultivating confidence advice from last month but more specific to speaking skills.
